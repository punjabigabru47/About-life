My name is John and living simple life saved my life

I used to be a pack rat. I had so many things which i thought i loved but the truth was i coudn't even appriciate any of it, because there was too much.

Things were horrible, job i hated and house i didnt want to live in and i coudnt hold the relationship.

>How i turned things around

I was on the verge of the breakdown when i got rid of everything. I litterly put 90% of my possessions in garbage and sold my place and moved into a smaller appartment, and i quit my job

It wasn't easy at all, but everything around me was in bad place. I didn't know what to do, and eventually in worked.

>Now i live the simple life

Now that im living simple life, things are much better now. i'm less stressed, enjoy my life and work more and i have more free time to enjoy.

With how much it turned my life around, I felt like i had no choice but start to sharring how i did it, and how others can benefit from living simple life as well, which is why i started this site.
